People, Culture and Values

This website summarizes, in a simplified way, how JPMorgan Chase publicly describes its approach to people, culture and workplace practices.

Topics include diversity and inclusion, employee benefits, expected conduct and official links where you can read more details.

Main HR Policy Themes

  • Encouraging a diverse and inclusive workforce across regions and roles.
  • Providing benefits that support health, financial security and work‑life balance.
  • Setting clear expectations for ethical behavior through a Code of Conduct.
  • Offering channels to raise concerns and protections for good‑faith reporting.

Purpose

The Code of Conduct describes how employees are expected to act with integrity, comply with laws and regulations and protect clients and the firm.

It applies to employees and, in many cases, directors and certain contractors associated with the company.

Workplace Behavior

The Code states that discrimination, harassment and retaliation are not tolerated and that everyone shares responsibility for a professional and safe workplace.

It connects with broader policies on equal opportunity and a respectful environment for colleagues and clients.

Speaking Up

Employees are encouraged to report concerns or potential misconduct through designated internal channels or hotlines.

Public documents explain that reports made in good faith should be free from retaliation, supporting a speak‑up culture.

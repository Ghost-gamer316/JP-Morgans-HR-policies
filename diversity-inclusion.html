Inclusive Workforce

Public materials highlight that JPMorgan Chase aims to build an inclusive culture where people from many backgrounds can succeed and contribute to better outcomes.

The firm links diversity of experience, skills and viewpoints with stronger decision‑making, innovation and long‑term performance.

Opportunities and Programs

Reports describe broad recruiting, mentorship and development programs that are used to attract and support a diverse talent pool globally.

Employee networks and resource groups are mentioned as ways to build community and support under‑represented groups in the workplace.

Respectful Environment

The Code of Conduct states that discrimination and harassment are not acceptable and that employees are expected to help maintain a respectful environment.

Equal Opportunity Statement

JPMorgan Chase states that it is committed to providing equal opportunity in accordance with applicable local laws in all areas of people management, including recruitment, hiring, promotion, compensation, benefits and training.[web:7]

Employment decisions are to be made based on legitimate business criteria and the qualifications, skills and experience of individuals, and the firm prohibits discrimination or harassment based on protected characteristics.[web:7][web:5]

Respectful Workplace

The firm’s Code of Conduct emphasizes maintaining a workplace free from harassment, intimidation and inappropriate behavior, and managers are responsible for addressing and reporting concerns.[web:1][web:10]

Employees are encouraged to speak up if they witness or experience conduct inconsistent with these expectations and are protected from retaliation when reporting in good faith.[web:1][web:10]

Source: JPMorgan Chase Equal Opportunity and Code of Conduct statements.[web:1][web:7]

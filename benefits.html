Health & Wellness

Public benefits guides describe medical, dental, vision and mental health coverage, with an emphasis on accessibility and predictable out‑of‑pocket costs.

Wellness programs and activities can provide additional support and may help employees manage healthcare expenses.

Retirement & Savings

For many U.S. employees, JPMorgan Chase offers a 401(k) savings plan with matching contributions up to a percentage of eligible pay after a period of service.

Some materials also mention automatic company contributions to retirement accounts for eligible employees, separate from employee contributions.

Time Off and Other Support

Summary documents and benefits sites mention paid time off, leave options, family and childcare support and other lifestyle benefits.

Benefits can vary by country, role and employment status, so employees are directed to official internal resources for exact details.

High‑level overview only. For full details, see the official benefits guides.
